Getting Started with CountMyStuff

1. Distributor List: Go to the Distributor List and add your distributors and their contact information. This ensures that when you generate orders, separate emails are generated for each distributor automatically.

2. Everything On Hand: Next, navigate to the Everything On Hand page. Here, add all products and ingredients you currently have in your business (e.g., PBR, Miller Lite, bags of sugar). For each item:

  • Set a Par Level so you’ll know when it’s time to reorder.
  • Select the appropriate Distributor for that item, so orders go to the right contact.

This step helps you maintain accurate inventory levels and automate order generation.

3. Do Your Counts (Count By Room): After adding items to Everything On Hand, go to Count By Room:

  • Add Rooms representing different areas of your business—e.g., Kitchen, Storeroom, Bar.
  • Within each room, Add Areas such as shelves or wells—for example, “Prep Rack,” “Speed Rail,” “Call Rail.”
  • Once areas are set up, you can begin adding items to each area:
    • Tap Add Item on an area to bring up a list of all items from Everything On Hand.
    • Select the items you have in that area. We recommend listing them in the order you will count them, you can always adjust the order of the list by dragging an item while in edit mode.
    • When you tap an item, you’ll enter the count screen. Use the +1 and –1 buttons to adjust, or enter a Custom Quantity directly. For open items like liquor bottles, kegs, or ingredients, use the Partial Increment buttons (10%, 20%, etc.).
    • Swipe left or right on the count screen to move to the next or previous item. This way, you can physically move down a shelf and swipe through items, making counting fast and efficient.

4. Generate Order: When you’ve completed your counts and you’re ready to reorder, go to the Generate Order page:

  • The app checks all items where your current Quantity is below the Par Level you set.
  • Items are grouped by their assigned Distributor, so each distributor gets a separate order.
  • At the top of the page, you can customize the e-mail subject. Enter your desired text in the “Email Subject” field and click Save. This setting persists across page reloads.
  • You can choose to Order in Cases or by individual units for each item by toggling the Use cases switch for each item.
  • When ordering by case, the Round up button adds one extra case for any item whose current case‐only quantity falls short of your par level but would exceed it if you ordered one more case.
  • Items not ordered by case show manual + / – buttons to adjust the order count more precisely.
  • If you need to start fresh, click the Reset All Changes button at the top of the page. You’ll be asked to confirm before all toggles and manual counts are cleared and saved.

Once your settings are in place, each distributor’s order appears in its table. You have two ways to proceed:

  • Copy Order: copies the order text to your clipboard, ready to paste into any message.
  • Generate E-mail: opens your default mail client with the distributor’s address, your custom subject, and the full order in the body.

Windows users: Before using Generate E-mail, make sure you have a default mail app set by going to Settings > Apps > Default Apps > Email and choosing your preferred client. If you go to a website to access your e-mail, you preferred client will be your browser. For example, Chrome, Firefox etc.

5. Export Your Data:

  • Generate clean PDF summaries of everything on hand as a single list, organized by room, or grouped by distributor.
